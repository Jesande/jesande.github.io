The brief

Create a digital strategy for Magis focusing on the optimization of the virtual configuration tool.

Magis is the Italian Brand focused on providing clients with high functional and technological quality products for the home, developed in partnership with major international designers.

Strategy proposal

Enhance the perception of the brand

Building a strong relationship between Magis and the user based on trust and accessibility by improving the users flow with a “fast, easy and intuitive” process, giving the proper information about the company and its products in order to give the user feel full control over the website.
