Digital Service Design

4 days workshop

The brief

Analyze the recent Artificial Intelligence developments in the Self-care topic (beauty & wellness) and ideate a digital service for skin focused people to improve the user experience in 4 ways: affective interactions, context-aware, predictive and deeply personalized services.

The research - User persona

Serena

Interior Designer, 45 years

  • Activities

  • Getting expert advice
  • Daily skincare routine
  • Face mask once a week

  • Needs

  • Find a proper expert advisor
  • Personalized product for the beauty routine
  • Suitable face mask

“Having a healthy skin makes me feel beautiful”

  • Challenges

  • Finding an advisor that works without spending much money and time
  • Remembering to do the mask

  • Oportunities

  • Give a user a personalized advisor based on its needs and location
  • Notify skincare activities

Beauty Mask is the only personal assistant skill that gives you personalized face mask ingredients for skin focused people

Beauty Mask Skill uses the GPS to detect weather and pollution of the user’s location and thanks to the AI identifies the possible factors that are affecting the user’s skin and provides the best possible solution to repair or prevent damages.
