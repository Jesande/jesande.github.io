The Fork app

The brief

Redesign The Fork app interface with the goal of increasing users’ adoption and usage.


Who is The Fork?

The Fork is an app/website that helps people find and book a table at a restaurant. It provides restaurant reviews, menu, pricing, hours and provides great deals (up to 50% off).

Who are the users?

  • Needs

  • Make a reservation online

  • Waiting for confirmation or availability of reservations”

  • Challenges

  • Unconsistent booking process
  • No proof of traditional cuisine
  • No option to modify the reservation
  • No Trust in people reviews

  • User friendly approach
